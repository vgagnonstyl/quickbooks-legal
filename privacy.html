Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This application ("Sync Invoices") is an internal tool developed and used exclusively by Styl Cabinets Inc. to retrieve invoice data from our own QuickBooks Online account via the Intuit API.

Data collected

This app accesses invoice data (invoice numbers, amounts, dates, customer names) associated with our own QuickBooks Online company account.

Use of data

Data retrieved is used solely for internal accounting and reporting purposes and is stored in our internal, private database. Data is not shared with any third party, sold, or used for any purpose beyond internal business operations.

Data security

Access tokens and credentials are stored securely and are not shared publicly.
